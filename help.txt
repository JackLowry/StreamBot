add_streamer <streamer username>
    Adds the streamer to the watchlist.

remove_streamer <streamer username>
    Removes the streamer from the watchlist.

set_home_channel
    Sets the home channel to where the message was sent. I will send updates when a watched streamer goes live to this channel.

list_streamers
    Lists all streamers on the watchlist.

currently_streaming
    Lists all streamers on the watchlist currently online.
